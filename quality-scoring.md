# Quality Scoring in Unstructured Data Extraction Pipelines

## Purpose

Quality scoring improves dataset reliability by measuring extraction confidence and identifying ambiguous signals.

Reliable datasets support stable routing and analytics workflows.

---

## Why Quality Scoring Matters

Extraction pipelines introduce uncertainty.

Confidence signals help:

detect errors
prioritize review
improve routing accuracy
stabilize analytics inputs

Quality scoring strengthens execution systems.

---

## Quality Signals

Common scoring indicators include:

entity confidence
classification certainty
missing-field detection
schema mismatch warnings

These signals guide remediation decisions.

---

## Example

entity extracted:

date: Tuesday

confidence score:

0.92

Low-confidence extractions may trigger review workflows.

---

## Review Strategies

quality scoring supports:

automatic acceptance thresholds
human review triggers
fallback routing paths
dataset filtering rules

Confidence-aware systems improve reliability.

---

## Outcome

Quality scoring enables structured pipelines to maintain high dataset integrity across evolving signal environments.
